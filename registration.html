Registration

Every accepted article should be registered by at least one author to appear in the proceedings. The acceptance list of articles will be announced on the website and the authors will be intimated regarding the acceptance through e-mail. The details about events and dates of registration are given below:

Registration and Camera-ready submission of the accepted articles Nov 2023
Author registration with late fees of INR 1600 (Indian authors) or $30 (foreign authors) Nov 2023

Registration includes ALL the following events to be completed by the author aforementioned deadline

A processing fee of INR 2500 will be deducted from the paid registration fee and the rest amount will be returned to the author/listener on 5-DEC-2023
